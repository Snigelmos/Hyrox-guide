Supplements for Hyrox Athletes

Hyrox is a uniquely demanding event — 8 km of running plus 8 functional stations lasting 60–120 minutes. The right supplements are not optional extras; they are a measurable edge.

Why supplements actually matter for Hyrox

Most fitness events are either a cardio test or a strength test. Hyrox is both — simultaneously. You're running hard enough to deplete glycogen while also doing repeated high-effort strength movements that tear muscle fibres. Your body is under a unique combination of metabolic stress that normal training nutrition doesn't fully address.

The good news: the science on a small number of supplements is very clear. They work, they're safe, and they're cheap. You don't need a shelf full of products. You need four things done right.

💪

Creatine

Station power
Grade A evidence

Increases phosphocreatine stores — the fuel for explosive, repeated efforts. Directly improves sled push, wall balls, burpees, and every other short-burst station. Effect: 5–15% more power output.

Full guide →
🥤

Protein

Recovery & muscle
Grade A evidence

Hyrox training is brutal on muscle tissue. Without enough protein (1.6–2.2 g/kg/day), you can't recover between sessions or adapt to training load. Recovery is where fitness is built.

Full guide →
⚡

Energy Gels

Mid-race fuel
Grade A evidence

At the 45–55 min mark your glycogen is running low. One gel = 25–30 g of fast carbs = enough fuel to sustain pace through the final four stations. Without it, you slow down or blow up.

Full guide →
🔥

Caffeine

Focus & endurance
Grade A evidence

200 mg of caffeine consistently delivers 2–4% improvement in endurance performance and reduces perceived effort. At race pace, that's 2–5 minutes. It's the most evidence-backed performance supplement that exists.
