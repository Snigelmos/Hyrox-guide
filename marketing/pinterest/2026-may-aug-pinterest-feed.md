# HYROX Vault Pinterest Feed — May 14 to Aug 5 2026

A single working document with 56 pin briefs, a 12-week posting schedule, and a copy-paste GPT image prompt for every pin.

Built for a brand-new Pinterest business account. Designed so you sit down once, generate 56 images in ChatGPT, schedule the whole batch in Tailwind or Metricool, and walk away for 12 weeks.

---

## How to use this document

1. Open ChatGPT image generation.
2. Paste the Master GPT Context (Section 2) **once** at the start of the chat. Keep that chat open for the whole batch.
3. Create the 5 Pinterest boards listed in Section 3.
4. Work through the schedule in Section 4 — for each pin, paste the per-pin image prompt from Section 5 into the same GPT chat, generate the image, and queue the pin in your scheduler.
5. Section 6 has scheduler setup. Section 7 is the review you do at week 12.

The pin briefs are written so you can copy `pin_title`, `pin_description`, and `overlay_text` straight into Pinterest (or your scheduler) without rewriting anything.

## 2. Master GPT image-generation context

Paste this **once** at the start of your GPT image-generation chat. Keep the chat open for the whole batch.

```text
You are helping create Pinterest images for Hyrox Vault, an affiliate/content website about HYROX training, workouts, gear, race prep, and beginner guidance.

The images are for a Pinterest business account. The goal is to drive qualified traffic to useful HYROX-related pages, not to create clickbait. The images should feel helpful, realistic, search-friendly, and suitable for people planning HYROX training.

Important context:
- Hyrox Vault publishes workout guides, benchmark sessions, training plans, race prep content, gear pages, and calculators.
- Some pages may include affiliate links, so the visuals must feel trustworthy and useful rather than salesy.
- Do not create fake product claims, fake official branding, fake testimonials, or fake event photos.
- Do not use official HYROX logos, event branding, copyrighted race signage, or fake sponsor logos.
- It is okay to create realistic HYROX-style training imagery: treadmills, SkiErgs, rowing machines, sled pushes, sled pulls, wall balls, farmers carries, sandbag lunges, burpee broad jumps, kettlebells, gym circuits, and race-prep scenes.
- The target viewer is usually a beginner or intermediate athlete researching HYROX workouts, training plans, or race preparation.

Image rules:
- Always create vertical Pinterest images in 2:3 ratio, ideally 1000x1500.
- The image must work as a mobile thumbnail.
- Use one clear idea per image.
- Leave space for large readable overlay text unless I ask you to include the text directly.
- Use realistic fitness photography or clean editorial illustration.
- Prefer modern gyms, training equipment, neutral backgrounds, high contrast, and clean composition.
- Avoid clutter, fake logos, fantasy colors, distorted hands, distorted equipment, unreadable text, plastic-looking people, and overly dramatic stock-photo lighting.
- If text is included, use large bold sans-serif text only. White or yellow on dark works best.

Brand tone:
Helpful, practical, serious but beginner-friendly. No hype phrases like "game-changer," "ultimate," "secret," "revolutionary," or "you won't believe."

When I give you a pin topic, create an image that matches the topic, the audience, and the overlay text. The image should make someone on Pinterest immediately understand what the pin is about.
```

## 6. Scheduler setup

Pinterest's native scheduler caps at 2 weeks ahead and ~100 queued pins, which won't carry a 12-week batch. Pick one of the following:

### Option A — Tailwind (recommended)

- Pinterest-approved partner.
- Smart scheduling suggests the best times based on your audience.
- ~$15 USD/month, free trial covers 100 pins.
- Works for the whole 56-pin batch in one upload session.

### Option B — Metricool

- Multi-platform (Pinterest, IG, TikTok, X, etc.).
- Free tier may cover this 56-pin volume.
- Cleaner UI than Tailwind for week-by-week planning.

### Option C — Pinterest native scheduler

- Free, built into Pinterest Business.
- Limit: 2 weeks lead time, max 100 scheduled pins.
- Use only if you want to schedule in 2-week chunks (re-open the doc every 2 weeks). Not recommended for this batch.

### Recommended workflow

1. Create the 5 boards in Pinterest (Section 3).
2. Create your scheduler account and connect it to your Pinterest business profile.
3. Open the Master GPT context (Section 2) in a fresh ChatGPT chat. Keep it open.
4. For each pin in the schedule (Section 4):
