# Pinterest — June 2026 Workout & Tips Batch (7 pins)

Continuation of the workout-tips series (the pillar driving HyroxVault Pinterest traffic). 4 sample-workout pins + 3 race-day tips pins, all routing to live `hyroxvault.com` pages. Every other day over two weeks.

- **Images:** branded workout cards with the actual session/tips rendered on the image. Files in `marketing/pinterest/images/`.
- **Image spec:** 1000 x 1500 PNG, photographic background + text panel.
- **Cost:** 7 background images at low quality = ~$0.14 total. Copy hand-written (no text-model spend).
- **Disclosure:** none of these pages carry affiliate buttons, so no paid-partnership toggle needed. Standard helpful/editorial tone.
- **Anti-spam:** unique image + title + angle per pin; boards rotated so no two consecutive pins share a board.

## Regenerating / editing the images

- Backgrounds: `python gen_backgrounds.py` (calls the local content API image endpoint at low quality).
- Cards: `python make_cards.py` (composites text from `CARDS` in `make_cards.py` onto `_bg/pin-0X.png`, outputs to `images/`).
- Single card: `python make_cards.py --only pin-03`.
- All copy on the images lives in `make_cards.py`; all pin metadata lives in this file.
