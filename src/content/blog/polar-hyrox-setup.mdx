Polar lacks an official Hyrox profile, but its HR accuracy and zone modelling are best-in-class. With a 5-minute custom activity setup in **Polar Flow**, you'll get a clean HR-zone breakdown of your race, arguably the most useful single data view for Hyrox.

## Best Polar watches for Hyrox

| Watch | Recommended? |
|-------|--------------|
| **Vantage V3** | ✅ Top pick, battery, HR, dual-band GPS |
| **Grit X2 Pro** | ✅ Great alternative |
| **Vantage M3** | ✅ Solid mid-range |
| **Pacer Pro** | ✅ Budget pick |
| **Vantage V2** | ✅ Older but works |
| **M430, V800** | ⚠️ Functional but dated |

All Polar watches with HR-zone tracking and manual laps will work for Hyrox.

## Building a custom Hyrox activity profile

### Step 1: Open Polar Flow on web or mobile

Polar Flow → Settings → Sport profiles.

### Step 2: Add a new sport profile

Click **+ Add sport profile** → Search → **Other indoor** → Name it "Hyrox".

If you have a Vantage V3 / Grit X2, you can also use **Multisport** for run + indoor segments.

### Step 3: Configure the profile

- **GPS:** Off (indoor; not needed)
- **Running cadence:** On
- **Heart rate:** Visible
- **HR zones:** Set to your personal zones (Polar calculates these from your HRmax)
- **Auto-lap:** Distance, every 1 km
- **Auto-pause:** OFF (very important, auto-pause messes up station timing)

### Step 4: Customise training views

Set up two views:

**View 1 (primary):**
- Heart rate
- Lap time
- Total time
- Distance

**View 2 (secondary):**
- Average HR
- HR zone (current)
- Calories
- Cadence

Save and sync to the watch.

## Step 5: Pair a chest strap

Polar H10 is the gold-standard HR chest strap. To pair:

1. Wet electrodes
2. Wear strap snug, just below sternum
3. Wake the watch
4. Settings → Sensors → Search & connect

The strap auto-connects when you start an activity.

## Race-day Polar checklist

Night before:

- ✅ Sync watch to Polar Flow
- ✅ Charge to 100%
- ✅ Confirm Hyrox profile is in your activity list
- ✅ Confirm chest strap battery is fresh

Race morning:

- ✅ Strap on chest HRM after breakfast
- ✅ Walk to corral with watch on wrist
- ✅ Start activity in corral, paused
- ✅ Resume + start at "GO"
- ✅ Manual lap button at each station entry and exit

## Polar's HR-zone advantage

Polar's strength is HR data. After the race:

- Polar Flow → Activity → HR section
- See **time spent in each zone**
- Compare against your training week

Polar's "Training Load Pro" calculates cardiovascular load + perceived load, which informs your recovery plan post-race. Most Hyrox athletes are at extremely high cardiovascular load (250-400 TRIMP points for a Hyrox).
