Coros lacks an official Hyrox profile, but its **simple lap structure** and **best-in-class HR battery life** make it a strong race-day choice. With a 5-minute custom workout in the Coros app, you'll capture clean HR data, manual station laps, and per-km running splits.

## Best Coros watches for Hyrox

| Watch | Recommended? |
|-------|--------------|
| **Vertix 2S** | ✅ Top pick |
| **Apex 2 Pro** | ✅ Great alternative |
| **Pace Pro** | ✅ Best budget |
| **Pace 3** | ✅ Solid budget |
| **Apex 2** | ✅ Works fine |
| **Pace 2** | ✅ Older but functional |

All Coros watches with manual lap and HR-zone tracking will handle Hyrox.

## Building a custom Hyrox activity

### Step 1: Open the Coros app on phone

Coros app → Profile → Activities.

### Step 2: Add or duplicate a profile

- For run + station combo: pick **Cross Training** (closest fit)
- For run-only logging: pick **Indoor Run** with manual segmenting
- For multi-sport setup: **Multisport** (Vertix/Apex 2 Pro only)

Rename to "Hyrox".

### Step 3: Configure data fields

Build a 4-field primary screen:

| Field | Position |
|-------|----------|
| **Heart rate** | Top |
| **Lap time** | Middle-left |
| **Total time** | Middle-right |
| **Distance** | Bottom |

Add a secondary screen with avg HR, max HR, and calories.

### Step 4: Set auto-lap

In the activity profile settings:

- **Auto-lap:** Distance, every 1 km
- **Auto-pause:** OFF (Hyrox stations would trigger it incorrectly)
- **Auto-scroll:** Off (manual swipe between screens)

### Step 5: Pair HR chest strap

Coros HRM-Fit pairs natively. Polar H10 also works (BLE-compatible).

1. Open Coros app → Devices → Add new device
2. Wet HRM electrodes, wear it
3. Confirm pairing

The HRM auto-connects when you start the Hyrox profile.

## Race-day Coros protocol

Night before:

- ✅ Sync watch to Coros app
- ✅ Charge to 100%
- ✅ Confirm Hyrox profile in activity list
- ✅ Update firmware if available

Race morning:

- ✅ Strap on chest HRM after breakfast
- ✅ Confirm watch shows HR before warm-up
- ✅ Start activity in corral
- ✅ Press lap (back-left button) at each station entry and station exit
- ✅ Stop watch at the finish line

That's roughly 16 manual laps across the race (8 stations × 2). It's doable but requires you to remember mid-race.

## Why Coros battery is a Hyrox advantage

A typical Hyrox lasts 60-100 minutes. Coros watches with chest-strap HR drain about 1-2% battery per hour in this mode. For multi-day Hyrox events (e.g. tracking warm-ups and the race), Coros' battery means you don't have to charge mid-event.

## Reviewing your race after

- Coros app → History
- Tap the activity
- View HR graph + lap-by-lap splits

The cleanest visualisation: filter by **HR zone breakdown** to see where your time was spent. For most Open Hyrox athletes, expect 60-70% of time in Z3-Z4.
