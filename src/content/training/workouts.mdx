## Grab a Workout and Go

No multi-week commitment needed. Pick a session, hit the gym, and train like a Hyrox athlete. We've organized these from full race simulations down to 20-minute blasts.

---

## Full Hyrox Simulations

### "The Full Send" — Complete Race Simulation

**Time:** 60–75 min | **Level:** Intermediate–Advanced | **Equipment:** Full gym

The closest you can get to race day without an entry fee. Complete every station in race order with 1km treadmill runs between each.

**Warm-up (5 min):** 800m easy jog, 10 leg swings each side, 10 arm circles, 5 inchworms

| Order | Exercise | Target |
|-------|----------|--------|
| 1 | Treadmill Run | 1km |
| 2 | SkiErg | 1,000m |
| 3 | Treadmill Run | 1km |
| 4 | Sled Push | 50m at race weight |
| 5 | Treadmill Run | 1km |
| 6 | Sled Pull | 50m at race weight |
| 7 | Treadmill Run | 1km |
| 8 | Burpee Broad Jumps | 80m (or 30 reps) |
| 9 | Treadmill Run | 1km |
| 10 | Rowing | 1,000m |
| 11 | Treadmill Run | 1km |
| 12 | Farmers Carry | 200m at race weight |
| 13 | Treadmill Run | 1km |
| 14 | Sandbag Lunges | 100m (or 50 each leg) |
| 15 | Treadmill Run | 1km |
| 16 | Wall Balls | 100 reps (men) / 75 reps (women) |

**Cool-down:** 5 min easy walk, full-body stretch focusing on quads, shoulders, and forearms.

**Pacing guide:**

| Station | Competitive | Average | Beginner |
|---------|------------|---------|----------|
| Each 1km run | 4:15 | 5:15 | 6:00 |
| SkiErg | 3:45 | 4:40 | 5:45 |
| Sled Push | 2:00 | 3:00 | 4:15 |
| Sled Pull | 2:00 | 3:00 | 4:15 |
| Burpee Broad Jumps | 4:00 | 5:15 | 7:30 |
| Rowing | 3:45 | 4:20 | 5:20 |
| Farmers Carry | 2:30 | 3:30 | 5:00 |
| Sandbag Lunges | 4:00 | 5:15 | 7:00 |
| Wall Balls | 4:00 | 5:15 | 7:00 |

**Scale it (beginners):** Cut runs to 800m, halve the burpee distance, reduce wall ball reps to 50, and use lighter sled/carry weights.

### "The Front Four" — First Half Simulation

**Time:** 30–40 min | **Level:** All levels | **Equipment:** SkiErg, sled, treadmill

Focus on the first four stations where most people go out too fast and blow up. Practice pacing here.

| Order | Exercise | Target |
|-------|----------|--------|
| 1 | Treadmill Run | 1km |
| 2 | SkiErg | 1,000m |
| 3 | Treadmill Run | 1km |
| 4 | Sled Push | 50m |
| 5 | Treadmill Run | 1km |
| 6 | Sled Pull | 50m |
| 7 | Treadmill Run | 1km |
| 8 | Burpee Broad Jumps | 80m or 30 reps |

**Key focus:** Start conservative on the SkiErg. If you go all-out on station 1, the sled push will destroy you. Aim for 75% effort on the first two stations.

**Scale it:** Reduce runs to 600m, use light sled weight, do 20 burpee broad jumps instead of 30.

---

### "The Back Four" — Second Half Simulation

**Time:** 30–40 min | **Level:** All levels | **Equipment:** Rower, kettlebells, sandbag, wall ball, treadmill

The back half is where races are won or lost. Your grip is gone, your legs are heavy, and you still have four brutal stations left.

| Order | Exercise | Target |
|-------|----------|--------|
| 1 | Treadmill Run | 1km |
| 2 | Rowing | 1,000m |
| 3 | Treadmill Run | 1km |
| 4 | Farmers Carry | 200m |
| 5 | Treadmill Run | 1km |
| 6 | Sandbag Lunges | 100m or 50 each leg |
| 7 | Treadmill Run | 1km |
| 8 | Wall Balls | 100 reps (men) / 75 reps (women) |

**Key focus:** Grip management. After rowing and farmers carry, your forearms will be screaming during lunges. Practice switching the sandbag shoulder every 25m.

**Scale it:** Reduce runs to 600m, carry lighter kettlebells, do 50 wall balls, lunge 50m instead of 100m.

### "Push & Grind" — Legs and Shoulders

**Time:** 30 min | **Level:** All levels | **Equipment:** Sled, wall ball, SkiErg

A circuit that hammers the push stations. Your legs and shoulders will be on fire.

**Warm-up (5 min):** 400m jog, 10 bodyweight squats, 10 push-ups, 20 shoulder circles

**The workout — 4 rounds, 90 seconds rest between rounds:**

| Exercise | Round 1 | Round 2 | Round 3 | Round 4 |
|----------|---------|---------|---------|---------|
| Sled Push | 50m | 50m | 25m | 25m |
| Wall Balls | 25 reps | 20 reps | 15 reps | 10 reps |
| SkiErg | 500m | 400m | 300m | 200m |

**Total volume:** 150m sled push, 70 wall balls, 1,400m SkiErg

**Scale it:** Use 60-70% of race weight on the sled. Beginners rest 2 minutes between rounds.

### "Grip & Carry" — Forearm Endurance

**Time:** 30 min | **Level:** All levels | **Equipment:** Sled with rope, kettlebells, sandbag, pull-up bar

Most people fail Hyrox because their grip gives out. This session trains the endurance your forearms need.

**Warm-up (5 min):** 400m jog, wrist circles, 30s dead hang, 10 light KB swings

**The workout — 4 rounds, 2 minutes rest between rounds:**

| Exercise | Reps/Distance |
|----------|--------------|
| Sled Pull | 50m at race weight |
| Farmers Carry | 100m at race weight |
| Sandbag Lunges | 50m |
| Dead Hang | Max time (aim for 30–60s) |

**Finisher:** 3 sets of towel pull-ups (max reps) or plate pinch holds (30s each hand)

**Scale it:** Reduce sled pull to 25m, carry to 50m, lunges to 25m. Use lighter weights. Dead hang can be done with feet lightly touching ground.

### "The Cardio Killer" — Maximum Heart Rate

**Time:** 30 min | **Level:** All levels | **Equipment:** SkiErg, rower, open floor space

Pure cardiovascular punishment. This one gets your heart rate to race-day levels and keeps it there.

**Warm-up (5 min):** 400m jog, 10 burpees at easy pace, dynamic stretching

**Part 1 — EMOM (Every Minute On the Minute) for 12 minutes:**
- Minute 1: 15 cal SkiErg
- Minute 2: 15 cal Row
- Minute 3: 6 Burpee Broad Jumps
- Repeat 4 times

**Part 2 — Intervals, 4 rounds:**
- 400m treadmill run at 90% effort
- 250m Row at max effort
- 60 seconds rest

**Cool-down:** 5 min easy walk, deep breathing

**Scale it:** Reduce EMOM calories to 10, burpees to 4 per minute. In Part 2, run 200m instead of 400m and row 150m.

## Quick Combos

### "Lunch Break Hyrox" — 20-Minute Blitz

**Time:** 20 min | **Level:** All levels | **Equipment:** SkiErg, rower, wall ball, floor space

When you only have 20 minutes but want to feel like you just raced. This is the one.

**No warm-up needed beyond the first easy round. Go.**

**4 rounds for time:**

| Exercise | Reps/Distance |
|----------|--------------|
| SkiErg | 250m |
| Rowing | 250m |
| Wall Balls | 10 reps |
| Burpee Broad Jumps | 10 reps |
| Treadmill Run | 400m |

**Target times:**
- Elite: under 16 min
- Competitive: 16–20 min
- Average: 20–25 min
- Beginner: 25–30 min

**No SkiErg or rower?** Substitute 30 cal assault bike for the SkiErg and 20 cal bike for the row. **No sled at home?** Heavy dumbbell bear crawls (10m) replace sled push. Towel rows on a smooth floor replace sled pull.

## How to Use These Workouts

Build your week around **3-4 sessions** using this template:

| Day | Session Type | Example |
|-----|-------------|---------|
| Monday | Station-focused | "Push & Grind" or "Grip & Carry" |
| Tuesday | Easy run | 5–8km at conversational pace |
| Wednesday | Rest or mobility | |
| Thursday | Station-focused | "The Cardio Killer" |
| Friday | Easy run | 5–8km + 4 strides |
| Saturday | Simulation | "The Full Send" or a half sim |
| Sunday | Rest | |

**Progression:** Start with half simulations and station sessions. After 3-4 weeks, do "The Full Send" once every 2 weeks. Track your times to measure improvement.

**Pair with supplements:** [Creatine](/supplements/creatine/) boosts your station power, [protein](/supplements/protein-powder/) speeds recovery between sessions, and [electrolytes](/supplements/electrolytes/) keep you hydrated during long simulation workouts.
