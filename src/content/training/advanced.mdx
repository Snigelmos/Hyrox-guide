## Competitive Edge: 12-Week Elite Plan

This program is for athletes chasing podium finishes or personal bests in the competitive field. You should already have multiple Hyrox races under your belt and be comfortable training 6 days per week.

**Prerequisites:** Sub-1:25 (men) or sub-1:35 (women) Hyrox time, consistent training base of 6+ months.

**Training schedule:** 6 sessions per week (3 running, 2 strength/stations, 1 race simulation)

**Target times:** Sub-1:10 (men) / Sub-1:20 (women) Open, or competitive Pro.

> **Chasing a sub-60 finish?** This advanced plan is built around 1:10/1:20 splits, not the elite 0:60 mark. If you are targeting a literal sub-60-minute finish, follow our dedicated [sub-60 Hyrox training plan](/training-plans/sub-60-hyrox-training-plan/) instead.

**Day 1, Long Run**
- 14–16km at easy pace (5:00–5:30/km)
- Include 4×30s hill sprints in weeks 3–4

**Day 2, Max Strength**
- Back Squat: 5×5 at 80–85% 1RM
- Romanian Deadlift: 4×6
- Weighted Pull-ups: 4×6
- Overhead Press: 4×6
- Sled Push: 5×25m at Pro weight (focus on explosive starts)

**Day 3, Threshold Intervals**
- 2km warm-up
- 5×1km at threshold pace (4:00–4:15/km) with 90s recovery
- 2km cool-down
- Build to 6×1km by week 4

**Day 4, Station Endurance**
Circuit (4 rounds, minimal rest between exercises):
- 500m SkiErg
- 25m Sled Pull at race weight
- 20 Burpee Broad Jumps
- 500m Row
- 100m Farmers Carry at race weight
- 50m Sandbag Lunges at race weight
- 25 Wall Balls

Rest 3 min between rounds.

**Day 5, VO2max Intervals**
- 2km warm-up
- 8×600m at 3:40–3:50/km pace with 2 min jog recovery
- 1km cool-down

**Day 6, Active Recovery**
- 30 min easy jog or bike
- Mobility work and foam rolling

**Day 7,** rest.

## Advanced Pacing Strategy

At this level, every second counts. The split below targets a **1:08 finish (Men Open)**. For a literal **sub-60 finish**, follow our dedicated [sub-60 Hyrox training plan](/training-plans/sub-60-hyrox-training-plan/), which is built around the elite sub-60 split structure.

| Sled Push | 1:40 |
| Run 3 | 4:15 |
| Sled Pull | 1:40 |
| Run 4 | 4:15 |
| Burpee Broad Jumps | 3:20 |
| Run 5 | 4:20 |
| Rowing | 3:25 |
| Run 6 | 4:20 |

Notice the **negative split on runs:** start controlled and push harder in the back half.

## Recovery & Performance Optimization

At this training volume, recovery is non-negotiable:

- **Sleep:** 8+ hours per night, consistent schedule
- **Nutrition:** 1.6–2.0g protein per kg bodyweight, adequate carbs for training volume
- **Supplements:** Creatine (5g/day), caffeine pre-race, electrolytes during training
- **Recovery tools:** Foam rolling daily, cold therapy 2×/week, massage monthly
- **Deload:** Every 4th week, reduce volume by 40% while maintaining intensity

## Full Supplement Stack for Advanced Athletes

At this training volume and competitive level, a complete supplement stack is worth the investment. Every supplement below has direct evidence for the demands of Hyrox:

- **[Creatine (5g/day)](/supplements/creatine/):** The most important supplement at any level. Consider Thorne Creatine (NSF Certified for Sport) if you compete in a tested division.
- **[Pre-workout / Caffeine (3–5mg/kg)](/supplements/pre-workout/):** Use a clean pre-workout with transparent dosing for hard training sessions. On race day, switch to simple caffeine tablets for precise dosing without GI risk.
- **[Energy gels (2–3 per race)](/supplements/energy-gels/):** At sub-1:15 pace, Maurten Gel 100 is the elite standard. Test your exact gel timing in race simulations.
- **[Electrolytes](/supplements/electrolytes/):** LMNT or Precision Hydration PH 1500 for heavy sweaters. Sodium-load starting 3 days before race day.
- **[Protein (1.8–2.0g/kg)](/supplements/protein-powder/):** At this training volume you need consistent high protein intake. Use a high-quality whey or blend to fill gaps between meals.
- **[Recovery: Omega-3 + Magnesium](/supplements/recovery/):** Daily omega-3 (2–3g EPA+DHA) and magnesium glycinate before bed are the two most impactful recovery supplements for high-volume athletes.
